ABOUT
Face multiple skeletons in a raid. Depending on the raid level, there will be more enemies with more damage and more health. Eliminate the skeletons by clicking all weak points on screen and complete the raid!
LOOT
If you successfully complete the raid, you get to keep all the loot and gold your character picked up.
ITEMS
There are different kinds of items and they come in different rarities. Every item collected is trackable and has a unique ID. Some items have a random generated colors.
MARKET
On the Market everyone can buy and sell items for gold.
HISTORY
Every item has a history. When it was first created in a raid and all the market trades it has been in. You can check the history of an item in your inventory or an item listed on the market.
LEADERBOARD
All players are listed on the leaderboard, sorted by current gold balance. By clicking a name you can view other players and what items they have equipped right now.
